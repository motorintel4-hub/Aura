# **App Name**: AURA AI: Kinetic Intelligence

## Core Features:

- Advisor Onboarding & Identity: Secure user authentication via Phone OTP, including profile setup with name, dealership, role, and profile photo.
- Guided 6P Customer Discovery: Step-by-step questionnaire capturing customer insights across Purpose, People, Pattern, Preferences, Price, and Purchase Context, with secure data entry.
- AI Buyer Persona & Briefing Tool: Automatically generates detailed buyer personas (e.g., 'Urban Family Buyer') and one-minute advisor briefings based on completed 6P data.
- AI Co-Pilot Pitch & Objection Handling Tool: Provides dynamic, context-aware AI recommendations for vehicle pitching at different levels (functional, emotional, aspirational) and strategies to overcome customer objections in real-time.
- Configurable Vehicle Battle Cards & Deal Builder: Access to comprehensive battle cards for four specific vehicle models, with competitive comparisons, and a deal configurator for adding accessories to build customer deals.
- Advisor Dashboard & Training Hub: Personalized dashboard displaying performance metrics, active sessions, and quick actions, alongside a 'Kinetic Training Hub' offering courses and co-pilot simulations.
- Customer Data & Document Management: Centralized customer profiles, journey timelines, and document storage, with support for notes, including AI-powered transcription for voice notes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Dark theme, with a deep charcoal near-black background and slightly lighter dark slate for surface cards, inspired by a cockpit aesthetic. The main background color is Dark Sepia (#1B1917) and surfaces are a slightly lighter shade (#2B2520).
- Primary action color: Electric Amber (#F2B80F) for interactive elements and highlights, suggesting energy and vibrancy.
- AI intelligence color: Kinetic Teal (#00B5B5) for AI co-pilot insights and interactive components related to generative AI.
- Badges for lead status: Hot leads in vivid Red (#CC2424), warm leads in Electric Amber (#F2B80F), and cold leads in a Cool Grey (#9B9FA3).
- Headline and section label font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a bold, wide-tracked, and modern techy feel, suitable for uppercase display.
- Body text and input fields font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a clean, readable, and neutral look that ensures clarity.
- Use abstract, motion-oriented icons for navigation and key actions, maintaining consistency with the 'Kinetic Intelligence' theme.
- Responsive layout with three breakpoints: single-column on mobile with a fixed bottom navigation; two-column grid on tablet with a collapsed left-side rail navigation; full three-column desktop layout featuring a persistent expanded sidebar navigation and a dedicated right-side AI Co-Pilot context panel.
- Smooth horizontal slide transitions between screens, and subtle upward fade-in animations for cards on load to enhance perceived responsiveness. The splash screen features an abstract, animated graphic suggesting speed and a cockpit HUD.
